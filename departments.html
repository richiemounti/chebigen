Math & science

Languages

Chebigen offers English and Kiswahili as compulsory languages. The language department is the most vibrant department in the institution and boasts several achievements.

About

Chebigen Secondary School was started on 1976. It is located in kericho county, ainamoi constituency. The school is funded by well wishers parents and the community development fund. It recently acquired a bus courtesy of the ainamoi Mp Hon Sylvanus Maritim.
